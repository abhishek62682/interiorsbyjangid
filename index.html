(Our Process)

A System That Doesn't Miss.

Every project follows the same disciplined 7-step process. No surprises. No re-work. No delays. This is the system behind every handover we've done in 50 years.

  1. 01

    Discovery Call

    Understand scope, budget, timeline & lifestyle requirements

  2. 02

    Site Visit & Measurement

    Detailed onsite survey with precision measurements & condition assessment

  3. 03

    Design Proposal

    2D layouts, 3D renders, material boards, BOQ & timeline presented

  4. 04

    Design Lock-In

    Final approvals, revision sign-off, payment schedule confirmed

  5. 05

    Production Begins

    In-house modular production starts parallely with site civil prep

  6. 06

    Supervised Execution

    Daily PM & supervisor presence. Milestone photos & updates shared

  7. 07

    Snag & Handover

    Formal snag list, final walkthrough, warranty documentation issued
